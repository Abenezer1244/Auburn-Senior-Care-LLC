The Attic Standard

Our methodology is built on three pillars that ensure dignity, safety, and happiness for every resident.

01

Holistic Assessment

Before admission, we conduct a deep-dive assessment not just of medical needs, but of personal preferences, history, and routines to create a truly personalized profile.

02

Dynamic Care Plan

Health isn't static. Our care plans are living documents, reviewed weekly by our nursing staff to adapt to changes in mobility, cognition, or medication requirements immediately.

03

Family Integration

We believe family is part of the care team. Through digital updates and open visitation, we ensure you are always connected and informed about your loved one's well-being.

Core Offerings

Daily Living Assistance

Personal Hygiene

Dignified assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting tailored to independence levels.

Nutrition Management

Three home-cooked meals daily plus snacks. We manage diabetic, low-sodium, and puree diets.

Medication Administration

Nurse-delegated staff manage prescriptions, refills, and precise timing for all medications.

Mobility Support

Transfer assistance (1-2 person or Hoyer lift), fall prevention monitoring, and exercise prompting.

Social Engagement

Structured activities, music therapy, gardening, and one-on-one companionship to prevent isolation.

Housekeeping & Laundry

Daily cleaning, fresh linens, and personal laundry service ensuring a pristine environment.

Clinical Excellence

Specialized Medical Support

We are equipped and certified to handle complex care needs that go beyond standard assisted living, allowing residents to age in place comfortably.

Dementia & Alzheimer's Care

Secured environment with wander management and cognitive support therapies.

Hospice & Palliative Care

Partnering with local hospice agencies to provide comfort, pain management, and dignity at end of life.

Diabetes Management

Blood glucose monitoring, insulin administration, and strict dietary planning.

Post-Stroke Recovery

Assistance with rehabilitation exercises and speech therapy homework.
