Our Story

Driven by compassion,
defined by excellence.

We believe that aging isn't about slowing down—it's about finding comfort in a place that truly feels like home. At Attic AFH, we blend professional medical oversight with the warmth of family.

Our Purpose

To create a sanctuary of dignity.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide senior care that prioritizes the individual. We strip away the institutional feel of traditional nursing homes and replace it with personalized attention, genuine relationships, and a safe environment where residents can age with grace.

  • Unwavering respect for individual autonomy
  • 24/7 Professional support with a personal touch
Our Vision

Redefining the standard of care.

We envision a future where "care facilities" are a thing of the past, replaced by care homes that integrate seamlessly into the community. We strive to set the benchmark for Adult Family Homes in Washington, demonstrating that high-level medical care can coexist with a cozy, domestic lifestyle.

  • Integrating advanced health technology
  • Fostering intergenerational community connections

Our Journey

A timeline of dedication and growth.

2018

The Foundation

Attic AFH was founded by Sarah Jenkins, an RN with 15 years of ICU experience, who realized that seniors needed more time and attention than hospitals could provide.

2020

Resilience & Safety

During global challenges, we implemented hospital-grade filtration and safety protocols, keeping 100% of our residents safe and healthy without isolating them from loved ones digitally.

2022

Memory Care Certification

Recognizing the growing need, our entire staff achieved advanced certification in Dementia and Alzheimer's care, transforming our home into a specialized memory support environment.

Today

Award Winning Care

Voted "Best Adult Family Home" in the county. We continue to expand our holistic offerings with music therapy, garden-to-table dining, and community integration.
